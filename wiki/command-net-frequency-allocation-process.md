This document formalizes the approved procedure for establishing, protecting, and utilizing the Command Net long-range (LR) radio frequency during BSG operations.

## 1. Purpose

This process ensures:

* The Command Net remains restricted to authorized personnel.
* Frequencies are not compromised through in-game voice or text communication.
* Radio discipline and operational security are maintained across all campaigns.

## 2. Authorized Roles

Only the following roles may access or transmit on the Command Net:

* Team Lead
* RTO
* JTAC
* Game Master

All other personnel must remain on their designated short-range squad radios unless explicitly instructed otherwise.

## 3. Frequency Agreement

The Command Net frequency for each mission is agreed upon privately by authorized roles via the designated out-of-game messaging platform **before mission start**.

The frequency must **never** be spoken aloud or typed in-game at any point during the mission.

## 4. In-Mission Procedure

1. Authorized personnel tune into the pre-agreed Command Net frequency before mission start.
2. The RTO confirms Command Net is active via a radio check with authorized roles only.
3. The actual frequency must never be spoken aloud or typed during the mission.
4. All other players remain on their designated short-range squad radios unless specifically instructed otherwise.

## 5. Compliance

All personnel in authorized roles are responsible for enforcing this procedure.
Any breach—intentional or accidental—must be reported to the Game Master team immediately so that a replacement frequency can be agreed out-of-game.

## 6. Review

This process will be reviewed periodically to ensure continued effectiveness and alignment with BSG operational standards.
